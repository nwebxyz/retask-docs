A **workspace** is the top-level home for your team in Retask. It holds your
projects, your people, your integrations, and your billing. Most teams need only
one. If you work with several separate clients or companies, you might keep one
workspace each so their work never mixes.

You switch between the workspaces you belong to using the **switcher at the top
of the screen** (it shows the current workspace name, like "NWEB Corp").

## Create and configure a workspace

You create your first workspace when you sign up. To create another, use the
workspace switcher and choose **Create workspace**.

Each workspace has a few basic details you can change any time under
**Settings → General**:

- **Name** — what your team sees in the switcher and at the top of the app.
- **Color** — a quick visual marker, handy if you belong to several workspaces.
- **Description** — an optional note about what the workspace is for.

## Members

Members are the people you invite into the workspace. Manage them under
**Settings → Members**, which has three tabs:

- **Members** — the people in your workspace and their roles.
- **Agents** — your AI Agents (covered in the [Agents guide](/guides/agents/)).
- **Invitations** — people you've invited who haven't joined yet.

![The workspace members list with names, roles, and join dates.](/screenshots/settings-members.png)

To invite someone, click **Invite member**, enter their email, and pick a role.
They'll get an invitation and show up under **Invitations** until they accept.

## Roles and permissions

Every member has one role. Higher roles can do everything the lower ones can,
plus more.

| Role       | View work | Create & edit tasks/projects | Manage members & settings | Billing & delete workspace |
| ---------- | :-------: | :--------------------------: | :-----------------------: | :------------------------: |
| **Viewer** |     ✓     |               —              |             —             |              —             |
| **Editor** |     ✓     |               ✓              |             —             |              —             |
| **Admin**  |     ✓     |               ✓              |             ✓             |              —             |
| **Owner**  |     ✓     |               ✓              |             ✓             |              ✓             |

<Aside type="tip">
  Pick the lowest role that lets someone do their job. You can always raise it
  later. **Editor** suits most teammates; reserve **Admin** for people who manage
  the team or settings.
</Aside>

## Deleting a workspace

Deleting a workspace is permanent — it removes every project, task, and setting
inside it, and it can't be undone. Only an **Owner** can do this, from the
**Danger zone** at the bottom of **Settings → General**.

<Aside type="danger">
  There is no recovery after deleting a workspace. Make sure you've exported or
  moved anything you want to keep first.
</Aside>
